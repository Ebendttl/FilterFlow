# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

FilterFlow contributors and maintainers are expected to keep collaboration respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the project.

Examples of welcome behavior:

- Being clear and kind in reviews and issues.
- Giving actionable feedback.
- Respecting different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Assuming good intent while still addressing concrete problems.
- Keeping discussions relevant to the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.
- Dismissing security or safety concerns without review.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior does not meet this standard.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner or maintainer with relevant context, links, and screenshots if available.

Reports will be reviewed with care and handled as confidentially as practical.
